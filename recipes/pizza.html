Pizza

Description

Crafting a perfect pizza begins with a flavorful dough made from a blend of all-purpose flour, yeast, salt, and a touch of olive oil. Start by activating the yeast in warm water with a pinch of sugar, letting it bloom until frothy. Mix this with the flour and knead vigorously until smooth and elastic, then allow it to rise until doubled in size. Meanwhile, prepare a robust tomato sauce by simmering crushed tomatoes with garlic, olive oil, and fresh basil until thickened and aromatic.

Roll out the dough into a thin, crispy crust, then generously spread the sauce over the surface. Top with a medley of sliced mozzarella cheese, pepperoni slices, saut√©ed mushrooms, and bell peppers for a burst of flavors. Finish with a sprinkle of grated Parmesan and a drizzle of olive oil before baking in a hot oven until the crust is golden and the cheese melts into gooey perfection. This homemade pizza promises a satisfying blend of textures and tastes that capture the essence of Italian comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups (280g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3/4 cup (180ml) warm water
  • 1 can (14 ounces / 400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 ounces (225g) fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced or shredded
  • Desired toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the Pizza Dough
  2. Make the Pizza Sauce
  3. Preheat the Oven
  4. Shape and Top the Pizza
  5. Assemble and Bake the Pizza
  6. Bake the Pizza
  7. Serve
